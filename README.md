# 🐾 SRUVO – AI Powered Pet Care Ecosystem

SRUVO is a modern pet-care ecosystem designed to simplify and enhance the way pet owners manage their pets' health, services, and daily activities.

Built with a strong focus on user experience, intelligent automation, and scalable product design, SRUVO combines AI-powered assistance, pet management tools, veterinary support, booking systems, and administrative controls into a unified platform.

---

## 🚀 Vision

Pet owners often struggle with managing appointments, tracking pet information, finding reliable services, and organizing daily care routines.

SRUVO aims to become a complete digital ecosystem where pet owners can seamlessly manage every aspect of their pet's life through a single platform.
